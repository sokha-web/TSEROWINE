ABOUT

Tsero Wine Company is a Georgian wine production company, which was founded in 2020. Its goal is to produce Georgian, natural wine. Currently, the wine is produced only from its own grape varieties, which is located in the Mukhrani Valley, in the area of the village Tserovni.

Particular attention is paid to strict observance of hygiene and sanitary norms, which is a prerequisite for healthy wine production. Tsero Wine's mission is to make a significant contribution to the development of the wine industry by producing the highest quality Georgian wine and to raise the reputation of Georgian wine in the international market.
